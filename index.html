Privacy Policy for Chimæra

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

Chimæra is a VPN application that routes selected app traffic through the Tor network to enhance your privacy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

Chimæra does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry
  • We do not collect crash reports
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not collect IP addresses
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not use third-party tracking services

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted:

  • Selected apps list: The list of apps you choose to route through Tor, stored in app preferences
  • VPN configuration: Your always-on VPN and kill switch preferences
  • Tor data: Tor circuit and relay information required for Tor to function, stored in the app's private data directory

Network Traffic

Chimæra uses Android's VpnService API to intercept network traffic from your selected apps and route it through the Tor network. This traffic passes through Tor relays operated by volunteers worldwide. Chimæra does not log, inspect, or store the contents of your network traffic.

Permissions

  • INTERNET: Required to establish Tor connections
  • VPN Service: Required to route app traffic through Tor
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep the VPN and Tor running
  • Boot Completed: Optional auto-start on device boot
  • Query All Packages: Required to display the list of installed apps for selection
  • Post Notifications: Required to show VPN status notification

Third-Party Services

Chimæra uses the Tor network (operated by The Tor Project) to route traffic. Please refer to The Tor Project's privacy policy for information about how the Tor network handles data.

The app optionally connects to api.ipify.org to verify your exit IP address when you use the "Check IP" feature. This is initiated only by user action.

Children's Privacy

Chimæra is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
